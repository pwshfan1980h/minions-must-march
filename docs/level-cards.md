# Minions Must March - Early Level Cards

These are review cards for the first puzzle batch. They are not final shipped levels yet; they define the intended challenge, tuning envelope, and preview-board shape.

## L001 - Bone Bridge

- **Biome:** Styx Marsh
- **Primary lesson:** click portal, assign one Builder, cross a flat/upward rib gap.
- **Jobs:** Builder x1
- **Skeletons:** 1 now; later 8, save 6.
- **Core atoms:** Portal Start, Builder Gap - Up.
- **Intended solution:** start portal, wait until skeleton reaches gold mark, assign Builder, walk into exit.
- **Failure mode:** no builder means Styx drop.
- **Tuning note:** keep as a clean mechanic demo until builder/vault behavior feels excellent.

## L002 - Turn, You Fools

- **Biome:** Styx Marsh
- **Primary lesson:** blocker reverses flow before danger.
- **Jobs:** Blocker x1, Builder x1
- **Skeletons:** 8, save 5.
- **Core atoms:** Safe Drop, Reverse Flow, Builder Gap - Flat.
- **Layout beat:** spawn spits skeletons down a short stair/drop onto a lower platform facing left toward soup. A blocker near the left lip turns the crowd right. A straight gap to the exit needs one Builder.
- **Intended solution:** start portal, place blocker after first skeleton lands, assign Builder to a right-facing skeleton near the gap.
- **Failure mode:** no blocker sends the crowd into Styx.

## L003 - Rib Ramp

- **Biome:** Crypt Forge
- **Primary lesson:** Builder can climb to a slightly higher platform.
- **Jobs:** Blocker x1, Builder x1
- **Skeletons:** 10, save 7.
- **Core atoms:** Reverse Flow, Builder Gap - Up, Delayed Release.
- **Layout beat:** crowd is reversed away from a forge trench, then one builder creates a rising rib ramp to a higher landing.
- **Intended solution:** blocker contains/reverses, builder starts from marked source lip, blocker release sends crowd over bridge.
- **Failure mode:** early builder too low misses the higher landing.

## L004 - Grave Slide

- **Biome:** Bone Gardens
- **Primary lesson:** downward builder bridges are forgiving but still need placement.
- **Jobs:** Builder x1, Blocker x1 optional/recovery
- **Skeletons:** 12, save 8.
- **Core atoms:** Safe Drop, Builder Gap - Down.
- **Layout beat:** skeletons descend a bone slope to a source platform; the exit sits on a lower far ledge across a Styx bite.
- **Intended solution:** assign Builder before the lip; bridge naturally slopes down into the landing.
- **Failure mode:** waiting too long lets the builder start too close to the drop.

## L005 - Two-Scoop Soup

- **Biome:** Styx Marsh
- **Primary lesson:** two builders across two short gaps with safe island staging.
- **Jobs:** Builder x2, Blocker x1
- **Skeletons:** 16, save 11.
- **Core atoms:** Island Staging, Builder Gap - Flat, Delayed Release.
- **Layout beat:** source platform, soup gap, tiny island, second soup gap, exit platform.
- **Intended solution:** first Builder to island, second Builder from island to exit, release crowd.
- **Failure mode:** spending both builders from the source side strands the march.

## L006 - The Bone Elevator Shaft

- **Biome:** Soul Cavern
- **Primary lesson:** safe vertical drop route; future Digger/uplight tone.
- **Jobs:** Blocker x1, Digger x1 later; for now can be a pre-open shaft prototype.
- **Skeletons:** 14, save 9.
- **Core atoms:** Safe Drop, Crumbly Floor Dig, Exit Uplight.
- **Layout beat:** top path dead-ends; marked crumbly floor leads to lower corridor and ghostly exit draft.
- **Intended solution:** contain crowd, dig/open shaft, let them fall safely and walk out.
- **Failure mode:** wrong route walks into dead end or hazard.

## L007 - Ashen Side Door

- **Biome:** Ash Catacombs
- **Primary lesson:** future Tunneler direction and cracked-wall readability.
- **Jobs:** Blocker x1, Tunneler x1 later, Builder x1 recovery.
- **Skeletons:** 18, save 12.
- **Core atoms:** Crumbly Wall Tunnel, Reverse Flow.
- **Layout beat:** exit is visible behind cracked catacomb wall; main path loops toward danger.
- **Intended solution:** reverse/hold crowd, tunnel through the cracked wall from the correct side.
- **Failure mode:** tunnel wrong direction or ignore wall cue.

## L008 - Hold the Line

- **Biome:** Crypt Forge
- **Primary lesson:** delayed release and crowd staging.
- **Jobs:** Blocker x2, Builder x1
- **Skeletons:** 20, save 14.
- **Core atoms:** Delayed Release, Builder Gap - Up, Soup Panic.
- **Layout beat:** one blocker protects the main crowd; another can redirect a worker. Builder prepares the route, then blockers are released in order.
- **Intended solution:** create a safe bridge before releasing the crowd.
- **Failure mode:** release too early spills skeletons into forge-soup.

## L009 - Broken Teeth

- **Biome:** Bone Gardens
- **Primary lesson:** step/vault chain over uneven terrain.
- **Jobs:** Builder x1, Blocker x1
- **Skeletons:** 12, save 9.
- **Core atoms:** Step Chain, Builder Gap - Up.
- **Layout beat:** uneven tooth-like platforms require smooth step-up behavior after a short bridge.
- **Intended solution:** build onto first tooth, then skeletons vault the rest.
- **Failure mode:** poor builder timing hits a tooth edge wrong.

## L010 - Three Ways Across

- **Biome:** mixed campaign test board
- **Primary lesson:** compare flat/up/down builder gaps in one normalized challenge.
- **Jobs:** Builder x3, Blocker x1
- **Skeletons:** 18, save 12.
- **Core atoms:** Builder Gap - Down, Flat, Up.
- **Layout beat:** same start and exit, three traversable lanes with different slopes. Useful as a tuning sandbox, not necessarily a shipped puzzle.
- **Intended solution:** any lane works if builder/vault tuning is robust.
- **Failure mode:** one slope feels unfair; use this board to catch it.
